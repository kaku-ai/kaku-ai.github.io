SMS/Messaging Terms

Last updated: February 6, 2026

These SMS/Messaging Terms ("SMS Terms") describe the text messaging program operated by Kaku ("we," "our," or "us") as part of the Kaku Event Coordination service. By using the Kaku app and its SMS features, you agree to these SMS Terms.

1. Program Name

Kaku Event Coordination

Kaku sends automated text messages to help coordinate group events such as brunches, dinners, movie nights, hiking trips, and other social gatherings.

2. What Messages You Will Receive

If You Are a Kaku User (App User)

As a Kaku app user, you may receive text messages related to:

  • Account verification and authentication
  • Plan status updates and confirmations
  • Service notifications

If You Are a Friend Being Coordinated With

If a Kaku user adds you as a friend and initiates event coordination, you may receive text messages that:

  • Introduce the event being planned and who invited you
  • Ask about your availability for proposed dates or times
  • Ask about your preferences (e.g., restaurant choices, activity options)
  • Confirm finalized event details
  • Provide updates if plans change

These messages are sent by an AI assistant on behalf of the Kaku user who is coordinating the event.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your event coordination activity. During active event planning, you may receive multiple messages as the AI assistant gathers availability and preferences. Outside of active coordination, you will not receive messages.

Typical message frequency:

  • Kaku users: 1-5 messages per week, depending on plans being coordinated
  • Friends being coordinated with: 2-10 messages per coordination event

4. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard messaging rates from your wireless carrier apply to all messages sent to and from Kaku. Contact your carrier for details about your messaging plan.

5. Opt-Out Instructions

You can stop receiving text messages from Kaku at any time.

To opt out, reply STOP to any message from Kaku. You will receive a one-time confirmation that you have been unsubscribed.

After opting out:

  • You will no longer receive SMS messages from Kaku
  • The Kaku user coordinating with you will be notified that you have opted out of SMS communication
  • You can opt back in at any time by replying START to the Kaku number

6. Help

For help with Kaku text messages:

Reply HELP to any message from Kaku to receive help information, or email us at support@kaku-ai.com.
