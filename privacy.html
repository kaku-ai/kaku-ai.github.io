Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Kaku ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Kaku mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App and related services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Authentication credentials

Contacts and Friends

To coordinate events, we collect information about the friends you add to Kaku:

  • Friend names
  • Friend phone numbers
  • Device contacts (read-only access, used to help you add friends more easily)

Conversation and Message Data

We collect and store:

  • Messages you send to the Kaku AI assistant
  • SMS messages exchanged between the Kaku AI and your friends during event coordination
  • Conversation history and context

Plan Details

Information about the events you coordinate, including:

  • Event type, dates, and times
  • Location preferences
  • Participant availability and preferences
  • Plan status (coordinating, confirmed, cancelled)

Device and Usage Information

We may automatically collect device type, operating system version, and general usage analytics to improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's event coordination features
  • Send SMS messages to your friends on your behalf for event coordination
  • Process conversations through our AI system to generate helpful responses and coordinate plans
  • Authenticate your account and maintain security
  • Improve and optimize the App's performance and features
  • Communicate with you about your account and service updates

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Kaku:

Supabase

We use Supabase for user authentication and database storage. Your account information, contacts, and plan data are stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Anthropic (Claude AI)

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to power the Kaku assistant. Your messages and conversation context are sent to Anthropic's API for AI processing. This includes messages you send to the assistant and messages received from your friends during coordination. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Twilio

We use Twilio to send and receive SMS messages for event coordination. Phone numbers and message content are transmitted through Twilio's messaging platform. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

4. Mobile Information and SMS Data

We will never share, sell, or distribute your mobile information, including phone numbers and SMS content, to third parties or lead generators for marketing or promotional purposes.

Phone numbers and SMS message content collected through our service are used solely for the purpose of event coordination as described in this Privacy Policy. This data is not used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to the Kaku service.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With third-party service providers as described in Section 3, solely to operate the App
  • To comply with legal obligations if required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect rights and safety if necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Kaku, our users, or others
  • With your consent when you explicitly authorize sharing

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained while your account remains active
  • Conversation and message data is retained for up to 12 months after the associated plan is completed or cancelled
  • Contact information for friends is retained while your account is active

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption in transit and at rest, secure authentication, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Request portability of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@kaku.app.

9. Children's Privacy

Kaku is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:
